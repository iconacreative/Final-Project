About

I was most recently providing design solutions at Starbucks as a senior designer with a focus on in-store promotions. In addition, I took on a 5-month time-limited assignment as a senior designer on Teavana to suppose their newly developed promotions team. Previously, I was a staff designer for FITCH Seattle, working closely with Microsoft Hardware. In that role, I was responsible for the conceptualization, art direction and execution of graphic communication, design and strategy as they relate to Microsoft packaging and merchandising objectives.
